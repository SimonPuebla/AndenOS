# Andén OS

**The operating system for Digital Economic Zones.**

Andén is onchain-native infrastructure that enables governments and zone operators to deploy and run Digital Special Economic Zones (DSEZs) — turning regulatory frameworks into executable, auditable software.

> 🇦🇷 Argentina MVP in active development · Zone 1 launching Q4 2026

---

## What we're building

There are 7,000+ Special Economic Zones globally. Almost none are built for Web3, AI, or frontier tech. They're paper-based, disconnected, and losing competitiveness to digital-first jurisdictions like RAK DAO or Singapore.

**Andén OS** is the infrastructure layer that changes that:

- **Digital incorporation** — entity registry and onboarding flows, fully digital
- **Automated compliance** — KYC/KYB/AML and eligibility rules enforced as software
- **Capital formation rails** — compliant tokenized fundraising and treasury management
- **Government dashboards** — real-time analytics, supervision tools, and governance controls
- **Composable modules** — identity, IP, and financial primitives as plug-and-play components

---

## Architecture

Andén OS is structured across five layers:

| Layer | Description |
|---|---|
| **Infrastructure** | Digital entity registry, identity + compliance SDKs, dashboards |
| **Deployment** | Turnkey kits for new DSEZs + legal and operational playbooks |
| **Operations** | Real-time analytics, supervision tools, rule updates, governance |
| **Components** | Specific apps (KYB/KYC, tokenization, treasury) for zone-specific use cases |
| **Use Cases** | Service providers operating within the zone: banks, VCs, networks |

## Why onchain

Onchain infrastructure enables:

- **Programmable compliance** — rules enforced and audited as code, not paperwork
- **Interoperability** — zones connect rather than each reinventing the stack
- **Transparent capital rails** — tokenized investment and treasury with full auditability
- **Composable innovation** — identity, IP, and financial primitives as standardized modules
